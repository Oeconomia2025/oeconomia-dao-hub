Stake OEC tokens to earn rewards and participate in protocol governance. Multiple staking pools are available with competitive APY rates and flexible lock periods. Includes a faucet for testnet tokens and an achievement rewards system.

[Launch Governance](https://governance.oeconomia.io/)

### <img src="https://pub-37d61a7eb7ae45898b46702664710cb2.r2.dev/images/Globe%20Black.png" alt="OECsplorer" width="28"> OECsplorer

## DeFi Protocols

### <img src="https://pub-37d61a7eb7ae45898b46702664710cb2.r2.dev/Eloqura.png" alt="Eloqura" width="28"> Eloqura DEX

A decentralized exchange for swapping tokens, providing liquidity, and managing positions. Supports both Eloqura native pools and Uniswap V3 routing for optimal pricing. Features automatic token discovery, real-time charts, and cross-chain bridging (coming soon).

[Launch Eloqura](https://eloqura.oeconomia.io/)

---

### <img src="https://pub-37d61a7eb7ae45898b46702664710cb2.r2.dev/With%20Border/ALUR%20With%20Border.png" alt="Alluria" width="28"> Alluria Protocol

A Liquity-style CDP lending protocol. Deposit ETH collateral to mint ALUD stablecoins, earn yield in the Stability Pool, and stake ALUR tokens for fee revenue. Features an emissions vault and on-chain price feeds.

[Launch Alluria](https://alluria.oeconomia.io/)

---

### <img src="https://pub-37d61a7eb7ae45898b46702664710cb2.r2.dev/images/OEC%20Logo%20Square.png" alt="Artivya" width="28"> Artivya

A decentralized marketplace for digital art and creative assets. Mint, trade, and showcase NFTs with built-in royalty support within the Oeconomia ecosystem. Currently in development.

[Launch Artivya](https://artivya.oeconomia.io/)

---

### <img src="https://pub-37d61a7eb7ae45898b46702664710cb2.r2.dev/images/OEC%20Logo%20Square.png" alt="Iridescia" width="28"> Iridescia

An innovative DeFi protocol bringing structured financial products to the Oeconomia ecosystem. Access vaults, yield strategies, and risk-managed investment options. Currently in development.

[Launch Iridescia](https://iridescia.oeconomia.io/)
